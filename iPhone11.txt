# # The iPhone 11 is a smartphone designed, developed, and marketed by Apple Inc.
#  It is the thirteenth generation of iPhone, succeeding the iPhone XR, and was unveiled
#   on September 10, 2019, alongside the higher-end iPhone 11 Pro at the Steve Jobs Theater in Apple Park,
#    Cupertino, by Apple CEO Tim Cook. Preorders began on September 13, 2019, and the phone was officially
#     released on September 20, 2019, one day after the official public release of iOS 13.
